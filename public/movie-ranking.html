The Best Movies According to Winny

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Kalki 2898

KALKI 2898 AD was outstanding! A visually magnificent sci-fi experience.
It had a little bit of “Blade Runner” & “Mad Max” in there as well.

Sita Ramam

An orphan soldier, Lieutenant Ram's life changes, after he gets a letter from a girl named Sita.
He meets her and love blossoms between them.
When he comes back to his camp in Kashmir,After he gets caught in jail, he sends a letter to Sita which won't reach her.

Agent Sai Srinivasa Athreya

Agent Sai Srinivasa Athreya is an authentic humorous investigative thriller revolving around the adventures of a detective based out of Nellore.
